Services overview

Dental Veneers

Veneers permanently fit over the front surface of a tooth to improve the shape, position and color of teeth, making them look healthier and more natural. With traditional porcelain veneers or Lumineers, you can expect a beautiful new smile in very little time.

Teeth Whitening

Your smile is one of the first things people notice about you, and having a whiter, brighter smile can help you make a positive first impression. With teeth whitening, we can brighten your smile by stripping away accumulated tooth stains and yellowing.

Oral Surgery

Unlike traditional surgery, oral surgery rarely requires a hospital stay. In fact, many procedures performed in our dental offices including tooth removal, wisdom teeth extraction, dental implants, and bone grafting are considered oral surgery.

Dental Bonding

Even if the rest of your smile looks great, you may have one chipped or darkened tooth that sticks out like a sore thumb. We can bring your natural smile to life by repairing your chipped, cracked or discolored tooth with cosmetic tooth bonding.
